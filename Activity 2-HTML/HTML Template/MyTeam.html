Product Manager

Greg Kacev and Simone Trubian are team Thundercats' Product Managers. The Product Managers define the product vision, strategy and roadmap; ensuring the team understands the business objectives behind what we build. They create adaptable roadmaps that align with changing priorities and guide delivery timelines. By engaging with customers and stakeholders, they make informed decisions about feature prioritisation to maximise value.

They coordinate closely with development, business and design teams to set clear expectations and ensure aligned delivery. Post-launch, they monitor product performance through metrics and user feedback, using insights to drive continuous improvement and iterate effectively. Their role acts as the bridge between business, technology and design; ensuring cohesive communication and aligned priorities across teams. For engineering, they streamline development through clear prioritisation and coordination. For business teams, they ensure their goals and customer needs are reflected in the product direction.

By setting clear goals, driving strategy and owning the product lifecycle, they help deliver meaningful outcomes, reduce misalignment and support the successful execution of initiatives across the organisation.

I interact with our Team's Product Managers (Greg & Siomone) on some occasions; to gain further clarity about specific initiatives I maybe working on.

Tester

Sai Kavuri is our team's only Tester Engineer. His role and responsibilities consist leading end-to-end testing of complex data flows spanning multiple interconnected systems; from the source system (RMS) to end-user platforms such as GOL and the Tu Website. He designs and executes component-level tests to validate the integrity of data ingestion through to downstream publishing.

Additionally, he provides cross-team support for testing, service migrations and upgrades, particularly where data exchange with our system is involved. He also integrates Static Application Security Testing (SAST) tools like SonarQube and Checkmarx to promote secure, high-quality, and maintainable code through continuous automated analysis.

Sai's work underpins the success of service migrations and application upgrades by ensuring seamless data integration and minimising risk. Through a strong focus on quality assurance and security, he helps the team deliver dependable and efficient software that boosts user confidence and operational reliability. The use of SAST tools facilitates early identification and resolution of vulnerabilities and inefficiencies, contributing to the delivery of robust, high-performing applications.
