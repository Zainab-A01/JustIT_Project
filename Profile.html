Ways I Prefer to Communicate

The following methods are ways in which to communicate with me:

    Email communication: I prefer using email for formal communication, detailed updates, or when sharing documentation that may need to be referenced later. I believe using email provides a clear and organised way to track important information and follow-ups. I currently only use my work designated email to communicate with colleagues outside of my team.

    Instant messaging: I tend to use instant messaging for quick questions, status updates, and day-to-day collaboration. Instant messaging allows for efficient, real-time communication without disrupting workflow, which I believe is ideal for staying aligned with my team at work. I use Slack and Microsoft teams to communicate daily with my team and any consumers who consume our services.

    Video calls: I find video calls particularly effective for team meetings, project discussions, work demonstrations/presentations and problem-solving sessions. They allow for more personal interactions, visuals, clearer communication and a quicker way for decision-making as compared to written messages. I currently attend video calls for the majority of our team meetings, such as our daily stand-ups, sprint retrospectives, sprint planning and ad-hoc meetings.

    Face-to-Face conversations: I believe face-to-face conversations also provide efficiency and the ability to foster stronger working relationships. In-person discussions are especially helpful for resolving complex issues or helps to align shared goals quickly among team members. We usually have face-to-face meetings every fortnight when myself and the team go to the office for our office working day.

How to Work with Me

The following are some tips on working with me effectively:

  • Communicate clearly and directly. Simple and direct messages help me understand what is needed quickly.
  • Give context when asking questions or assigning tasks. Knowing the background of the topics at hand, helps me do my work more accurately and efficiently.
  • Set clear expectations and deadlines. This helps me plan effectively and helps me stay on track with priorities.
  • Keep me informed of changes. Early communication of changes allows me to make adjustments for better outcomes.
  • Ask questions and share ideas. I value open dialogue and believe collaboration leads to better results.
  • Share feedback. I value constructive feedback and continuous improvement as this helps me enhance the quality of my work.
